### プロンプト3：上級テクニック編
**タイトル**：【Suno AI】物語を紡ぐ！「BGM＋ボーカル」で映像作品に命を吹き込む
**用途**：特定のシーンや雰囲気に合わせたBGMとボーカルを同時に生成し、映像作品や朗読劇などに活用できる楽曲を作成します。
**プロンプト本文**：
```
You are a skilled songwriter and composer for film scores. Create a piece that evokes a sense of mystery and wonder, suitable for a scene where a protagonist discovers an ancient, hidden artifact in a forgotten temple.

Part 1: Instrumental BGM
Style: Orchestral, cinematic, with a touch of ethereal synth.
Mood: Mysterious, awe-inspiring, slightly tense, building anticipation.
Instrumentation: Strings, subtle choir, deep percussion, atmospheric pads.
Dynamics: Starts soft and sparse, gradually builds to a powerful, climatic moment.

Part 2: Vocal Melody
Lyrics:
(Whispered)
Dust motes dance in silent air,
A secret held, beyond compare.
The carvings glow with ancient light,
Unveiling truths within the night.

(Sung, ethereal female voice)
Oh, the whispers of the ages past,
A forgotten path, forever cast.
This sacred place, where shadows creep,
Promises the world will keep.

(Building intensity)
The power stirs, a dormant flame,
Calling out my whispered name.
What lies within this hallowed ground?
A destiny unbound!

Instructions for Suno AI:
Generate an instrumental track first, following the BGM specifications.
Then, integrate the provided vocal melody and lyrics, ensuring they blend seamlessly with the instrumental, particularly during the build-up. The vocal should feel like an integral part of the discovery and awe.
```
**使い方のコツ**：AIに役割（"You are a skilled songwriter and composer for film scores."）を与え、BGMとボーカルを明確に分けて指示します。BGMのスタイルやムード、楽器編成、そしてボーカルの歌詞と歌い方を指定することで、より複雑で意図的な楽曲制作が可能になります。特に、映像作品への活用を想定する場合に有効です。
